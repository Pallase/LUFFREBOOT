General Information

Since the 1980’s, Luff’s focus has always been the design, development, and manufacture of high performance frequency synthesizers, phase-locked oscillators, and complex assemblies that deal with frequency generation or conversion. Our years of experience gives us the prime ability to implement new designs, emphasizing quality, reliability, and state-of-art performance.

Frequency Multipliers

Previous Next

Luff Research offers a full line of active RF/Microwave x2 and x4 multipliers. These units' can multiply frequencies to the 8 to 34 GHz frequency range and are fitted in connectorized and rugged assemblies. In addition, all of our multipliers are system ready with other Luff Products, but can also be used as stand-alone components for other various microwave frequency generation, making Ku-band and K-band operation possible at a lower cost.

Phase Locked Crystal Oscillators

Previous Next

Luff Research's Low Noise Crystal Oscillators are the lowest phase noise frequency sources available. Using high quality crystals, excellent phase noise and harmonic/sub-harmonic performance is achieved anywhere from 10 MHz to 1400MHz. In addition, these units are fitted in a rugged and economical solution for many high-performance applications in commercial wireless and aerospace/military industries.
