General Information

Since the 1980’s, Luff’s focus has always been the design, development, and manufacture of high performance frequency synthesizers, phase-locked oscillators, and complex assemblies that deal with frequency generation or conversion. Our years of experience gives us the prime ability to implement new designs, emphasizing quality, reliability, and state-of-art performance.

Quality Assurance

Quality is forged into our products by our team of dedicated professionals who perform and monitor all aspects of design and production.

RoHS Compliance

Luff Research provides, upon request, frequency source products in full compliance with EU Directive RoHS 2005/95/EC.

Conflict Minerals Policy

Luff Research is committed to the protection of human rights and the promotion of high ethical business standards.
