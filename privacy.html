General Privacy Policy Statement

Protecting your privacy is an integral part of our customer service program. Luff Research protects your online activity according to the security measures described below, and we never share your e-mail address or other personal information with anyone.

Information Collection

Luff Research will only collect personally identifiable information (such as name, title, company name, address, telephone number, or e-mail address) that you voluntarily provide through our website or e-mail correspondence.

Use of Information

The information you supply will be used by Luff Research to respond to your inquiries, supply you with requested information on Luff Research products and services, or track orders you place with Luff Research. If you have submitted personal information to Luff Research electronically and would like it removed or modified, please contact us at sales@luffresearch.com. We will use reasonable efforts to comply with your request.

Other Important Information

The Luff Research website may contain links to other sites. We do not endorse or otherwise accept responsibility for the content or privacy policies of those sites. Additionally, some of our web pages may contain “cookies,” which are data that may be sent to your web browser and stored on your computer. This allows our server to “recognize” you when you visit our website in the future. Most web browsers can be configured to not accept cookies, or to notify you if a cookie is sent to you.

It may be necessary, if required by law or if pertinent to judicial or governmental investigations, to release your personally identifiable information.

Luff Research reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. We will promptly reflect any such modifications here.
